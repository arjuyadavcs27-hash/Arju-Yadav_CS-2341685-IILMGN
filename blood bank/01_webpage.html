About LifeFlow

Our Mission

To ensure a safe and sufficient blood supply for patients in need through voluntary blood donation.

Our Team

A dedicated team of healthcare professionals working to make blood donation safe and accessible.

Our Impact

Serving the community with reliable blood supply and saving countless lives every year.

Why Donate Blood?

Benefits of Blood Donation

  • Reduces the risk of heart disease
  • Helps maintain healthy iron levels
  • Free health check-up
  • Saves lives

Donation Process

  1. 1 Registration and health check
  2. 2 Medical history review
  3. 3 Blood donation (10-15 minutes)
  4. 4 Refreshment and rest
